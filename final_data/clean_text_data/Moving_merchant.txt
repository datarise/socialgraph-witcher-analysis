This Merchant is one of the few that'll "travel" to different locations. He is always present in one the following locations, though not always when the player passes through them:  In Blackbough In between Carsten and Vegelbud Residence At the crossroads, east of Glory Gate and northwest of Cunny of the Goose Also, at each location he offers a different conversation option. He sells some components to crafting, but most of his wares are animal hides. He does not play gwent.    The merchant icon is always present on the map, at the location where he was last seen by the player (even if he is present at a different location).

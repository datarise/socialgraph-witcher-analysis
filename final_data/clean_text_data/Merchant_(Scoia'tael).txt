This merchant is an elven member of the Scoia'tael commando hiding out in the forest clearing south of the Loggers' Hut, along with some other Scoia'tael soldiers. He typically sells a few swords, food, glyphs, crafting components, and some crafting diagrams. He also plays gwent and, if beaten, gives the Cirilla gwent card.    Gwent: Big City Players    The elf claimed to have met Geralt in the Brokilon forest once.

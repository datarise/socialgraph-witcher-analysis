Henry var Attre was a noble who served as a diplomat to Novigrad for thirteen years, first as consul and later as ambassador. He lived there in his Var Attre Villa in the rich Gildorf district. He informed Geralt of the political situation in the Northern Kingdoms during the witcher's visit to Vizima. He was the father of both Edna and Rosa var Attre.

Yontek was said to be biggest fool in the village of Lurtch located in central Velen. Despite this, he wasn't greedy like the other villagers, which actually resulted him being the only one not turned into a pig when the rest stole from an underground altar.    Fools' Gold    He can be found sometimes with his hand glowing and waving it around, suggesting he is capable of magic. However, this could just be a bug or an oversight.

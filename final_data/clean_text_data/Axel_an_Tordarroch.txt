Axel an Tordarroch was a Skelliger warrior from Undvik, somehow related to the erstwhile Jarl Harald Houndsnout, whom he served as one of his personal guard. When the ice giant attacked, Axel tried to reach the jarl, but he had to face some sirens and passed out during the fight. Shortly later, a longship with a few warriors guarding children and women brought the unconscious Axel to Ard Skellig, where he then frequented The New Port tavern.    The Lord of Undvik

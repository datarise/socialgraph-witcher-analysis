The Incredible Lockhart was a merchant and dice poker player who can be found under the arch in Loc Muinne's central square during chapter III.    Lockhart was previously a student at the Ban Ard Academy from which he was expelled as a result of his drinking and gambling. Upon his arrival at the Loc Muinne summit, he found himself ostracized from the magical community, and began to renounce his profession by trying to sell off his magical items, such as grimoires and amulets.    Beating The Incredible Lockhart at dice can net one of several rewards:  Gold A design for the magic wrap armor enhancement 1 fire rune and 1 earth rune 1 runic armor enhancement    Expelled from the Ban Ard magical school for carousing and gambling, Lockhart was selling his magical tomes and amulet at quite moderate prices.    Poker Face: Loc Muinne    His name is in reference to Gilderoy Lockhart, a famous wizard in the Harry Potter universe who is actually a fraud, taking credit for everyone else's work.

Jonas is an innkeeper that can be found at New Port Inn in Kaer Trolde Harbor. He was born in Undvik, where he ran "The Old Port". This was before the Ice Giant arrived, which led to the exodus of the local Skelligers of Clan Tordarroch. He will sell four gwent cards:  Botchling Earth Elemental Eredin: Commander of the Red Riders Scorch (1 of 3)    The Lord of Undvik

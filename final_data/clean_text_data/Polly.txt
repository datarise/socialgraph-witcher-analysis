Polly was a choreographer in Novigrad in 1272.    In 1272, Dandelion contacted Polly to recruit her for his planned cabaret at the premises of the former Rosemary and Thyme. However, Polly's betrothed Hubio was against the idea of her working there, believing the Rosemary and Thyme to still be a brothel per the old publicity placards which he came across. If Geralt chose to help Dandelion in setting up the cabaret, they went to Polly's house and settled a domestic dispute between Polly and Hubio, after which she started choreographing shows at the Chameleon.

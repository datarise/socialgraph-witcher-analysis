Marica served as the eyes of the blind Thecla in 1272 at Bald Mountain.    During the 1272 Crones' Sabbath, Marica allowed Geralt and Ciri to see Thecla, who selects candidates for meeting the Crones. After Geralt and Ciri were accepted for entry, Marica escorted them to the large doors which lead into the interior of Bald Mountain.    Bald Mountain

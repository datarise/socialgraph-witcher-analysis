Patrick Hazelnutt was a resident of Novigrad in 1272, and a gwent player.    In 1272, Hazelnutt participated in the gwent tournament at the Passiflora organized by Count Tybalt. He was eliminated in the first round after playing against Harald Featherbuns. Hazelnutt was accompanied at all times by three female paramours, whom, it was said, he is never seen in public without.    High Stakes

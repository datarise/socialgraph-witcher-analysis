Duke and his associate, Earl are Gwent card collectors and minor thief captains willing to use intimidation to get the cards they want. When you ask Zoltan Chivay on his topic, he will say that after the fall of Whoreson Junior he is rising in the Novigradian underworld.    A Dangerous Game    After a chase, Geralt can finally corner Duke in the sewers where he can either let him go or "finish him". If he kills him however, the man can still be found later, apparently neither dead nor undead, in the same spot, though he has little to say.

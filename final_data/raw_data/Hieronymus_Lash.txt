|Hair_color = Black {{Small|text=beard}}}}'''Hieronymus Lash''' was a [[mage]] loyal to [[Count]] [[Linus Maravel]].

== Biography ==
During the times of the original [[Chapter of the Gift and the Art|Chapter]], Hieronymus was caught practicing [[Forbidden art|black magic]] and considered dangerous ever since.

In {{Year|canon = no|year = 1271}}, after the sudden assassination of King [[Foltest]], he and Maravel came up with a plan to remove [[House of La Valette|La Valettes]], get Baron [[Ravanen Kimbolt]], arrested and make Count Maravel the new king, however by the blessing of [[Nilfgaardian Empire|Nilfgaard]]. In [[Loc Muinne]], Hieronymus worked as a messenger between Nilfgaardians and Maravel.

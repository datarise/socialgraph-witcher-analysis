'''Evald''' was one of six guardsmen watching over the outermost gate leading to [[Kaer Trolde citadel]]. He, his friend and shieldmaiden can be seen talking about various topics, such as who will become the next [[King of the Skellige Isles|King]] after the death of [[Bran Tuirseach]], and when will next raid occur and so on.

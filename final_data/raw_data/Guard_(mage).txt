The '''Guard''' was a [[vran]] [[mage]] entrusted with the protection of the [[forgotten vran sword]] hidden in an [[Crypt of Mages|ancient crypt]] under [[Loc Muinne]]. To do so, the Guard wielded a powerful staff which helped him with [[teleportation]], raining [[fireball]]s, creating [[Protection (defensive spell)|defensive shields]] and summoning [[wraith]]s.

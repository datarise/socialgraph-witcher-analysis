'''Spark''' was an [[Elf|elven]] merchant who lived in [[Vergen]], on [[Rhundurin Square]], who sold food items. She was the only Vergen merchant available to [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] if he choose [[Vernon Roche|Roche]]'s path.

==Notes==
* She is likely named after [[Iskra]], one of the [[Rats]].

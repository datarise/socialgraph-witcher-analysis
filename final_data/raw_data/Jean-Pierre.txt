|Appears_games = {{Tw1}}}}[[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] first meets '''Jean-Pierre''' on [[Landing in the swamp|the landing]] in the [[swamp]] as part of [[The Wolf Contract]] in Chapter II. Jean-Pierre hopes that bringing in these [[Wolf pelt|wolf pelts]] will secure him the coveted position of [[Royal huntsman|Royal Jägermeister]], otherwise he plans to become a mercenary. He also relates a brief tale of [[Leo Bonhart]] (a character from the Witcher series) to Geralt. This is however, not the last time they meet.

The two meet once more in [[Old Vizima]], in Chapter V, as part of [[Mud and Velvet]] and [[The Bruxa Contract]]. Jean-Pierre is also [[Antoinette|Antoinette's]] fiancé, whom Geralt meets at the [[Foltest's castle|Royal castle]].

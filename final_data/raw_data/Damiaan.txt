'''Damiaan''' was a [[Redania]]n archer who served on [[HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor]], protecting [[King]] [[Radovid V]].

In {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}}, he and his comrades, [[Caspar]], [[Jouke]], [[Freek]] and witch hunter [[Egbert]] were commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [[St. Gregory's Bridge]] and secure the area. He died by the hand of either [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]], [[Ves]], [[Roche]] or some [[Temeria]]n partisan.

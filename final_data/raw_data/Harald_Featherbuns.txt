'''Harald Featherbuns''' was a resident of [[Novigrad]] in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, and an avid [[gwent]] player.

==Biography==
In 1272, Featherbuns participated in a [[High Stakes|gwent tournament]] at the [[Passiflora]] organized by Count [[Count Tybalt (Novigrad)|Tybalt]]. In the first round, he was pitted against [[Patrick Hazelnutt]] and won, however he lost in the second round when he played against [[Finneas]].

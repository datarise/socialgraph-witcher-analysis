'''Agda''' was a [[Skellige]]r woman from [[Hindarsfjall]], and the fiancée of [[Timmon]].

== Biography ==
In {{Year|year=1272|canon=yes}}, [[Ulf (Timmon's brother)|Ulf]] and [[Mikkel (Skelliger)|Mikkel]], Timmon's brothers, picked her up and were on their way back to [[Faroe Isle|Faroe]] when a storm hit, causing their ship to crash and Agda unfortunately drowned.

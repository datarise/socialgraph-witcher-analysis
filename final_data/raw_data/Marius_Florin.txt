'''Marius Florin''' was a [[Redania]]n aristocrat and a close friend of the [[Vegelbud family]], as well as a passionate of [[Gwent]] using the [[Monsters Gwent deck|Monsters deck]]. It is said that he never loses thanks to his [[zeugl]]-bone amulet.

[[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] can play with him during the masquerade ball at the [[Vegelbud Residence]] and will receive the [[Dandelion (gwent card)|Dandelion card]] if he beats him.

'''Igor''' was one of [[Cyprian Wiley|Whoreson Junior]]'s employees and the administrator of the [[Whoreson Junior's arena|arena]] of [[Novigrad]].

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt goes to the arena alone }} Igor and all his men were killed by [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] in {{Year|canon = No|year = 1272}} inn his search for Whoreson Junior.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt helps Cleaver's men}} Fearing an upcoming war between him and Whoreson Junior, [[Carlo Varese|Cleaver]] decided to strike first, and thus sent his [[Crownsplitters|gang]] and Geralt to raid Whoreson Junior's main hideouts, the [[Whoreson Junior's casino|casino]] and the arena, where Igor and all his men were killed.

'''Marcus''' is a secondary character in {{Tw2}}. He is a [[The Witcher 2 merchants|merchant]] who can be found with [[The Mighty Numa]], southeast of the tower near [[Loc Muinne]]'s central square during chapter III.

Although he sells some [[The Witcher 2 potions|potions]], the most interesting item in his shop is the [[Anabolic steroids]].

== Journal Entry ==

'''Dirk''' was a cruel and sadistic torturer who worked for the [[witch hunter]]s. One of his favorite ways to torture mages was taking a fingernail, or as he said, "better three". He died when [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] and [[Triss]] destroyed the [[Witch Hunter Barracks Outpost|witch hunters' outpost]] in [[Novigrad]] in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}.

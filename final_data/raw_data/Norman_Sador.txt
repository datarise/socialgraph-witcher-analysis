'''Norman Sador''' was a [[Temeria]]n crossbowman and a veteran of the [[Battle of Brenna]] and the Pontar Wars.

== {{Tw2}} ==
During the Prologue, just before full charge on [[La Valette Castle]], King [[Foltest]] recognized and promoted him to corporal of crossbowmens for his long service to Crown. After that he appears no more.

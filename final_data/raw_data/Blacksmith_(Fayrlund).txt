This '''blacksmith''' was a [[Skellige]]r who lived in [[Fayrlund]].

He loved to play [[gwent]], using the [[Monsters Gwent deck|Monsters deck]].

==Biography==
In {{Year|year=1272|canon=no}}, he was captured by bandits who imprisoned him at the foot of the cliff below Fayrlund.

{{Condition|Text=If Geralt saved him}} [[Geralt of Rivia]] saved him from his cage, and after a day, the blacksmith was back to business in the village, he was able to sell a unique [[crossbow]], [[:Category:The Witcher 3 weapons|swords]], [[Runestone|runestones]], [[The Witcher 3 crafting components|crafting components]] and also some [[:Category: The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams|diagrams]].

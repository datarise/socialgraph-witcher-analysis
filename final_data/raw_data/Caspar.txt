'''Caspar''' was a [[Redania]]n archer serving on [[HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor]] to protect [[King]] [[Radovid V]] in the year {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}.

He and his comrades, [[Damiaan]], [[Jouke]], [[Freek]] and witch hunter [[Egbert]], were commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [[St. Gregory's Bridge]] and secure the area. He died by hand of either [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]], [[Ves]], [[Roche]] or some [[Temeria]]n partisan.

'''Vincent Molnar''' was a [[knight]] at the [[The Witcher 2 Arena|arena]] in [[Temeria]], who [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] could hire, to assist him in a fight for 500. When talking to him he mentioned a woman he was courting who would only have him if he slayed a [[manticore]] and was at the arena to meet this condition.

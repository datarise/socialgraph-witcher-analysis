[[Margrave]] '''Vladimir de Cret''' was a powerful [[Redania]]n magnate and aristocrat, with close ties to the [[Vegelbud family]]. He was also a recognized [[gwent]] player who was not particularly averse to losing, given his wealth.

== {{Tw3}} ==
Vladimir de Cret may be encountered during the masquerade ball at the [[Vegelbud Residence]], where he is participating in a gwent contest. He gives the [[Milva (gwent card)|Milva]] gwent card if beaten.

He uses the [[Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck|Nilfgaardian Empire deck]].

'''Valgard''' was a fistfighter in [[Skellige]] in {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}}.

He can be found just outside the door to [[Yennefer of Vengerberg|Yennefer]]'s room at [[New Port Inn]] in [[Kaer Trolde Harbor|Kaer Trolde Harbour]].

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt fights him}} Knowing he had no chance against a [[witcher]], he talked to [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] in private, asking him to throw the match so he could impress [[Eryka]], a woman he was interested in.

:{{Condition|Text = If Geralt loses the first match}} Valgard thanked the witcher as Eryka approached, clearly impressed with his fighting ability.
:{{Condition|Text = If Geralt doesn't throw the first match}} Miffed, Eryka showed her disappointment in Valgard and walked off, despite Valgard calling after her.

'''Dilvyn''' was a regular soldier in [[Imperial Army of Nilfgaard]] about to be lynched by some farmers. [[Geralt of Rivia]] had the option to let the peasants proceed or to intervene and save the man. If Geralt left the man to his fate, he could loot his corpse and retrieve a [[Letter from Nilfgaardian's wife|letter from his wife]].

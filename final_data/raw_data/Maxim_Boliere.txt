'''Maxim Boliere''' is an actor residing in [[Novigrad]]. He belongs to the troupe of [[the Butcher's Yard theater]]. Maxim has a drinking problem and is usually seen with a bottle in his hand outside the theater. [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] can choose either him or [[Abelard Rizza]] to play the role of the [[doppler]] during the quest [[The Play's the Thing]].

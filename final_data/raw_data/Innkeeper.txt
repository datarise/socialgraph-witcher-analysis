Throughout the games, there are several '''innkeepers'''.

== {{Tw1}} ==
There are several '''innkeepers''' in {{Tw1}}. They can all arrange for [[The Witcher character interaction|storage and accommodations]], and some will even impart some useful information for the right price: 
* Innkeeper Olaf in the [[Outskirts Inn|Outskirts]]
* Innkeeper at [[The Hairy Bear]]
* Innkeeper at [[The New Narakort]]
* Innkeeper in [[Country Inn|Murky Waters]] (male)
* Innkeeper in [[Country Inn|Murky Waters]] (female)

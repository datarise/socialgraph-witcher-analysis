'''Jethro''' was a member of the [[City Guard]] in the [[Temple Quarter]] and the jailer at the [[Vizima dungeon|dungeon]]. He worked for [[Vincent Meis]] officially, but seemed to have quite the little enterprise on the side as well. He had a strong addiction to [[fisstech]] which cause him to act delirious at times.

== [[The Witcher journal|Journal]] Entry ==
:''Jethro is a guard in the city dungeon. He would merely be a standard smartass, except that he's addicted to fisstech.''

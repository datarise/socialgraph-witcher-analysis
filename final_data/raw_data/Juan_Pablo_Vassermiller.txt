'''Juan Pablo Vassermiller''' was one of the royal [[Ebbing quadruplets]] along with his siblings [[Putzi]], [[Gritzi]], and [[Mitzi]]. When they were just small children [[Dandelion]] described them collectively as "horrible, trouble-making brats".<ref>{{SoD}}</ref>

In {{Year|canon=no|year=1271}}, he was visiting [[Vizima]], capital of [[Temeria]], and attended a banquet held in Princess [[Adda the White|Adda]]'s name at the [[The New Narakort]].<ref>{{Tw1}}</ref>

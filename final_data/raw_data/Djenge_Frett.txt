'''Djenge Frett''' was a [[bounty hunter]] in [[Skellige]] around {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}} who considered himself the most famous bounty hunter north of the [[Yaruga]].

== Biography ==
In 1272, he found himself scouring Skellige for the [[Grossbart Brothers]], a trio who had done so many misdeeds a bounty had been placed on their heads.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt helps him}} with the [[witcher]]'s help, Djenge was able to bring an end to the brothers' reign of terror.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt doesn't help him}} unfortunately Djenge overestimated himself and tried to take on the brothers by himself and met his end.

== Trivia ==
* His name and profession are both a reference to [[wikipedia:Jango Fett|Jango Fett]], a bounty hunter in the ''[[wikipedia:Star Wars|Star Wars]]'' series or to Django, another bounty hunter in the movie [[wikipedia:Django Unchained|Django Unchained]].

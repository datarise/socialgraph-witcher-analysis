'''{{PAGENAME}}''' was an [[Alchemist|alchemist]] and generally shady character who asked Geralt to get him the formula for a particular type of incense which is manufactured and distributed by a [[Vilmos Bartok|vendor]] in [[Flotsam]].

He can be found hanging out near the docks early in Chapter I.

* [[The Scent of Incense]]

* Vencel Pugg seems to be a member of [[Salamandra]].

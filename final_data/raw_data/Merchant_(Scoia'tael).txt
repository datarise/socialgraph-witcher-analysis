This '''merchant''' is an [[elf|elven]] member of the [[Scoia'tael]] commando hiding out in the forest clearing south of the [[Loggers' Hut]], along with some other Scoia'tael soldiers.

He typically sells a few swords, [[The Witcher 3 food and drink|food]], [[glyphs]], [[:Category:The Witcher 3 crafting components|crafting components]], and some [[:Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams|crafting diagrams]]. He also plays [[gwent]] and, if beaten, gives the [[Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon (gwent card)|Cirilla gwent card]].

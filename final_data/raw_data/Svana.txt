'''Svana''' was a [[priest]]ess of [[Freya]] and, around {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}}, the race organizer for [[Skellige]]'s races, known collectively as [[The Heroes' Pursuits: For the Goddess' Glory!|The Heroes' Pursuits]]. She stationed herself in [[Larvik]] where everyone who wished to compete placed a horseshoe on the table beside her.

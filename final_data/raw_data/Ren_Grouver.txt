}}'''Ren Grouver''' was a [[dwarf]] and member of the [[Scoia'tael]] who worked for [[Yaevinn]].

He can be found in the [[Vizima sewers]] of the [[Trade Quarter]], if you get the [[Echoes of Yesterday]] quest and you will meet him again at the [[Vivaldi Bank|bank]] during the [[Gold Rush]] quest.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt attacks the Scoia'tael during Gold Rush}} He and the Scoia'tael robbers were killed by [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]].

Marius Florin was a Redanian aristocrat and a close friend of the Vegelbud family, as well as a passionate of Gwent using the Monsters deck. It is said that he never loses thanks to his zeugl-bone amulet. Geralt can play with him during the masquerade ball at the Vegelbud Residence and will receive the Dandelion card if he beats him.

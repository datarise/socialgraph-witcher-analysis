La Valette Guard Captain was an old and experienced soldier who served Baroness Maria Louisa La Valette as commander of her guard around her villa in Novigrad.    In 1272, he met Geralt of Rivia when the witcher came looking for Maria Louisa. While he originally objected to their meet, Maria and Morvran Voorhis came out of their own accord to meet Geralt.    Broken Flowers    Unlike the other guards in the game, you are unable to attack the captain or members of the La Valette guard.

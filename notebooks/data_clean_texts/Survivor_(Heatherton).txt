The unnamed Survivor was the only one who survived when Heatherton was attacked by the Wild Hunt in 1272. However, he was so distraught by what happened to everyone else that when Geralt approached to find out what happened to the Nilfgaardian spy, Hendrik, he had to use Axii to calm the survivor down.

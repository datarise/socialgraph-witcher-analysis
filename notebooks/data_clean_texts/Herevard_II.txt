Herevard II (Polish: Hereward II) is the successor of Hereward as Prince of Ellander. He was always considered the rightful heir and as such also obtained a proper education. When Nilfgaardians conquered Vizima, he decided to not object to his new ruler and "fell in line with the new order."    Imperial Audience    The name "Hereward" is derived from the Old English elements here "army" and weard "guard". This was the name of an 11th-century Anglo-Saxon leader who rebelled against Norman rule. The v instead of w in English translation is for better pronunciation.

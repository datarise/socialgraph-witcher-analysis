Raven is a legendary witcher mentioned in The Witcher. In particular, he was known to have possessed a splendid armour which was forged in the gnomes' workshops beneath Mount Carbon, and finished by elves. This armour was buried with him. He is first mentioned by Berengar. Inscription on his tomb states:  Here lies the witcher known as Raven - a great warrior and defender of the weak. Honored be his name. Since Raven's tomb cover is broken, one can see that he is actually buried with two witcher's swords.    Armor Raven's armor

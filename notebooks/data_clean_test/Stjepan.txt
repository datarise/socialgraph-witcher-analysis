Stjepan was The Alchemy's innkeep and a gwent player, using the Nilfgaardian Empire deck. If beaten, he'll give the Yennefer gwent card. On top of the usual food and drink, he also sells three gwent cards:  Dwarven Skirmisher (10 ) Mahakaman Defender (20 ) Vrihedd Brigade Veteran (20 )    Gwent: Playing Innkeeps Contract: The Oxenfurt Drunk    If Aldert Geert is beaten at gwent in the Prologue, he recommends playing Stjepan, praising him as a "true maestro" in gwent.

Liglad was the captain of one of the ships docked in Novigrad's port. If approached to sail to Skellige: he refused to sail Geralt to the islands, being wary of being attacked by Skelligers and Nilfgaardians alike, and thus advised the witcher to go find Captain Wolverstone at the Golden Sturgeon instead.    Destination: Skellige    In Danish, "Ligeglad" means "indifferent".

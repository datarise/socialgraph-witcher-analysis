The  blacksmith in Murky Waters does not live alone, his clan boasts three members, two of them merchants:  the blacksmith himself his wife his son    The blacksmith is a very versatile fellow. He dabbles in many things and has wide-ranging interests, not just weapons and armour. He can be of use for forging new meteorite and rune swords and for general repairs. He also buys some surprising things for a blacksmith:  wyvern steaks some herbs some potions (Wives' Tears, White Raffard's Decoction) weapons (actually buy them, not just make them) blade coating bases alcohol gifts (including clothing, flowers and gems) books and scrolls and not to forget fisstech    Armour The Heat of the Day Temptation

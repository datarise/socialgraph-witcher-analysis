Hieronymus Lash was a mage loyal to Count Linus Maravel.    During the times of the original Chapter, Hieronymus was caught practicing black magic and considered dangerous ever since. In 1271, after the sudden assassination of King Foltest, he and Maravel came up with a plan to remove La Valettes, get Baron Ravanen Kimbolt, arrested and make Count Maravel the new king, however by the blessing of Nilfgaard. In Loc Muinne, Hieronymus worked as a messenger between Nilfgaardians and Maravel.    The Messenger

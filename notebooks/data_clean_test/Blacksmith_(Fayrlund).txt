This blacksmith was a Skelliger who lived in Fayrlund. He loved to play gwent, using the Monsters deck.    In 1272, he was captured by bandits who imprisoned him at the foot of the cliff below Fayrlund. If Geralt saved him: Geralt of Rivia saved him from his cage, and after a day, the blacksmith was back to business in the village, he was able to sell a unique crossbow, swords, runestones, crafting components and also some diagrams.

Margrave Vladimir de Cret was a powerful Redanian magnate and aristocrat, with close ties to the Vegelbud family. He was also a recognized gwent player who was not particularly averse to losing, given his wealth.    Vladimir de Cret may be encountered during the masquerade ball at the Vegelbud Residence, where he is participating in a gwent contest. He gives the Milva gwent card if beaten. He uses the Nilfgaardian Empire deck.    A Matter of Life and Death

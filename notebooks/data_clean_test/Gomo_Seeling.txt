Count Gomo Seeling was a Redanian aristocrat and a close friend of the Vegelbud family as well as a passionate of Gwent, using the Scoia'tael deck. This love for gamb however didn't bring much fortune to his family since both him and his father lost their houses in the Nimnar valley for their debts. Geralt can play with him during the masquerade ball at the Vegelbud Residence and will receive the Bruxa card if he beats him.    A Matter of Life and Death

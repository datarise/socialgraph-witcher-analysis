The elven craftsman was part of Toruviel's band of starving elves who camped in the cave by the Lakeside in Murky Waters. He was looking for four pieces of centipede armor for his work and was willing to pay. He was also a master craftsman, though the term slightly offended his artistic nature, and was capable of doing many things: mirror reassembly being one such skill.

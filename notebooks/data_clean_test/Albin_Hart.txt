Albin Hart was a scavenger working in Velen in the year 1272, where he looted the bodies of dead soldiers on battlefields to try and make ends meet. If Geralt helps him and he survives: Albin was grateful and rewarded the witcher for his help. His brother-in-law also rewarded the witcher with a discount on a counterfeit pass. If Geralt helps him and he dies: Unfortunately the necrophages proved too much and Albin was killed by them while trying to loot the corpses on the battlefield.    Bitter Harvest    If Geralt helps him and he survives, the Shady merchant will sell the transit pass to Geralt for 25 .

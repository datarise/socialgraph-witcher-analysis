Loan Shark was a merchant in The Bits district of Novigrad. While he normally just gave out loans, he did sell a few items like the book Change your life! A Handbook. He also played gwent, using the monsters deck.    At some point, the merchant came into business with the pirate Morkvarg from Skellige. He gave the man a loan, and the two went into business often. If Geralt lets Morkvarg live: In 1272, on Morkvarg's instructions, Geralt of Rivia came to the loan shark's pawnshop and retrieved an alchemy formula and the sword Deithwen, which Morkvarg had left there.

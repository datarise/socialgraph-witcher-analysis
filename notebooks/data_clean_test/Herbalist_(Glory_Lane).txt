This halfling Herbalist has a shop off Glory Lane, which is located across the canal to the south of Hierarch Square in Novigrad. He sells the quest item "bundle of burdock", in addition to a number of ingredients for alchemy and the following manuscript pages:  Manuscript page: Enhanced Thunderbolt Manuscript page: Enhanced Northern Wind Manuscript page: Enhanced Devil's Puffball Manuscript page: Enhanced Maribor Forest Manuscript page: Enhanced Petri's Philter He is also a steady gwent player, using the Scoia'tael deck.

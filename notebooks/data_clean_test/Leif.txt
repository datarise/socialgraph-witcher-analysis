Leif was the father of Kori and Kraki and part of Clan Drummond in 1272. He was considered to be a troublemaker who detested visitors from the continent, a view he made sure to pass on to his sons. If Kori and Kraki are killed: despite his sons being troublemakers themselves and having instigated the deadly fight that cost them their lives, Leif demanded that the lone survivor, Geralt, be tried by their jarl, Madman Lugos, for his sons' deaths.

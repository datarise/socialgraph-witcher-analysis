Piet is a minor character and Redanian Soldier who appears in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt in the end of mission, The Great Escape. He is a member of the Town Patrol of Oxenfurt and assigned to Deireadh prison. He is one of numerous guards tasked with safeguarding outer ring of prison from any possible intruders. Even though he is not fond of mages, he feels pity for sorceresses and sorcerers tortured by a witch hunters inside of the prison because, as he says, at the end they are also humans.

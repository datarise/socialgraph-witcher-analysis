The Archpriest was high-ranking cleric in 1271 who run the ancient Monastery on grounds of La Valette Castle.    In April 1271, when nobles of northwestern Temeria spearheaded by Maria Louisa La Valette revolted against Foltest, the archpriest sided with them. After the rebellious army's defeat, Foltest, Geralt of Rivia and Vernon Roche met him once they manage to reach the Monastery in order to learn where the royal children were taken. Geralt hexed the archpriest with Axii to reveal they are in the solar.    To the Temple!    This church dignitary, confident of his god's protection, greeted the intruders with harsh words. Yet he succumbed to persuasion and revealed where the king would find his illegitimate offspring.

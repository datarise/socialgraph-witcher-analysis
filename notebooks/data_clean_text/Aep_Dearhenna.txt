A mage using the name aep Dearhenna was studied the decline of the Vrans and had a laboratory in Loc Muinne. This laboratory plays an important part in The Secrets of Loc Muinne. He created the Talking Golem, was famous for his thousand genies curse and the Prototype Megascope.

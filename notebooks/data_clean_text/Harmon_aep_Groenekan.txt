Lieutenant General Harmon aep Groenekan, commander of Second Mounted Army of Nilfgaard, was a connoisseur and collector of armors. During 1272, he demanded that the armorers at Crow's Perch in Velen make him a suit, but insisted that the finished product be tested before paying. This evolved into small contest of skills between Fergus and Yoana.    Master Armorers

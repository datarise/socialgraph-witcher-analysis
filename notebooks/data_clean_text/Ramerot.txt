Ramerot and his brothers Corbin and Buse were noblemen from Beauclair and Antoinette's cousins. During Chapter V they were in hiding in the refugee caves in the swamp cemetery after incurring the disfavour of the King Foltest and they were completely out of orens. Ramerot himself is in the southernmost refugee cave, in the chamber next to the boxer, Zdenek.    Mud and Velvet

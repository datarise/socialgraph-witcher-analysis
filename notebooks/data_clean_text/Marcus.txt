Marcus is a secondary character in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. He is a merchant who can be found with The Mighty Numa, southeast of the tower near Loc Muinne's central square during chapter III. Although he sells some potions, the most interesting item in his shop is the Anabolic steroids.

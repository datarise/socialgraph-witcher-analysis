Happen the Eunuch was an assistant of Sigi Reuven and the bookkeeper of the bathhouse of Novigrad, where he had the assignment of receiving guests and writing their names in a ledger. According to his boss Dijkstra, he helped Happen escape from a Kovirian prison the night before his execution, thus the reason for Happen's undying loyalty to his boss. In turn, this caused Dijkstra to trust his assistant completely as he believed saving one's life meant they wouldn't turn against him.    Get Junior Count Reuven's Treasure

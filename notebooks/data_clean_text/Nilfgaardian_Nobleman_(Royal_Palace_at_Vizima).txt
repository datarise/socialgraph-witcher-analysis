This unnamed Nilfgaardian nobleman is a character in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt temporarily residing in the Royal Palace at Vizima, his wealth, power, and social influence put him in an influential position in the court of Emhyr var Emreis. Players are recommended to play with him as soon as they are able to beat him to obtain his gwent card as he may not be there later. He plays the Nilfgaardian Empire deck - a true patriot. If beaten, he awards the player with Foltest: The Siegemaster (gwent card).    Collect 'Em All

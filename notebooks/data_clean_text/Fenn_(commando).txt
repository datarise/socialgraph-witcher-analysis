Fenn was a member of the Blue Stripes, an elite Temerian Special Forces Unit in 1271. He enjoyed a good drink, arm wrestling and fistfighting. Fenn shared the fate of the Blue Stripes and died by the order of Dethmold.    Fenn's body is missing from the tent where the Blue Stripes were hanged. Same goes for Thirteen and Silas.

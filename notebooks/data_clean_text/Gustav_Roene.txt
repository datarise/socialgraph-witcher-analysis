Gustav Roene was an opponent at Whoreson Junior's arena in 1272. He can be the first opponent one faces if they choose to fight in the arena and would yield once defeated, giving the option to finish him off or spare him. If spared, he will fight alongside Geralt against a pack of dogs, the Hairy Brothers, a wyvern, endrega drones, and finally Boris.    Get Junior The Gangs of Novigrad

'''Freek''' was a [[Redania]]n archer serving on [[HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor]] to protect King [[Radovid V]].

==Biography==
In {{Year|year=1272|canon=no}}, while the HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor was docked in the [[Novigrad Docks]], he and his comrades, [[Caspar]], [[Jouke]], and [[witch hunter]] [[Egbert]] were commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [[St. Gregory's Bridge]] and secure the area. He was killed by the hand of either [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]], [[Ves]], [[Roche]] or some [[Temeria]]n partisan.

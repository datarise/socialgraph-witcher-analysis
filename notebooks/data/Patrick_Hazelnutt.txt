'''Patrick Hazelnutt''' was a resident of [[Novigrad]] in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, and a [[gwent]] player.

==Biography==
In 1272, Hazelnutt participated in the gwent tournament at the [[Passiflora]] organized by Count [[Count Tybalt (Novigrad)|Tybalt]]. He was eliminated in the first round after playing against [[Harald Featherbuns]]. Hazelnutt was accompanied at all times by three female paramours, whom, it was said, he is never seen in public without.

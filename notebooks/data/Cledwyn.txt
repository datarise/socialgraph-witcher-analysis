'''Cledwyn''' was the imperial [[barber]] of [[Emperor]] [[Emhyr var Emreis]] and subordinate of [[Mererid]], the emperor's chamberlain. He was the who shaved off [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]]'s beard, when the [[witcher]] visited the emperor in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, while [[Morvran Voorhis|General Voorhis]] asked some questions about recent events.

== Trivia ==
* Morvran Voorhis interviews Geralt while being shaved, explaining that "men turn honest when they feel a blade at their throat."
* In-game, the floating text only names him "barber".

'''Finneas''' is a racist [[half-elf]] and one of the contestants in the [[Gwent]] tournament at the [[Passiflora]]. Geralt plays him if he first beats [[Bernard Tulle]] and [[Carthia van Canten|Sasha]]. If [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] defeats Finneas, the half-elf accuses the witcher of cheating and fisticuffs break out.

He uses the [[Scoia'tael Gwent deck|Scoia'tael deck]].

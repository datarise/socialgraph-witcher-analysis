'''Bjorg''' was a master shipwright in [[Kaer Trolde Harbor]] during {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}} and where he was training his apprentice, [[Rurik]]. Finally fed up with what he perceived were just [[drowner]]s killing people near the cliffs, he decided to find someone to take care of the problem.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt accepts the contract}} He informed [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] the two known witnesses he remembered seeing the monster's attack: his apprentice, Rurik, and a widow named [[Tove]].

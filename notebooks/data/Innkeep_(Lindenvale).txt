There are actually two '''innkeeps''' in [[Lindenvale]]. Both work in the [[Lindenvale tavern|tavern]] behind the village's noticeboard, one during the day, and one during the evening.

The night innkeep a few [[The Witcher 3 alchemy ingredients|herbs]], some [[The Witcher 3 food and drink|food and drink]], and [[pure silver]] on the other hand the day innkeep sells herbs, powdered pearl, and pure silver.

== Notes ==
* Night innkeep looks exactly like [[Marcus T.K. Hodgson]], the bookseller in [[Novigrad]].

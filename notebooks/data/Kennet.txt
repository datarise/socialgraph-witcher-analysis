'''Kennet''' was the youngest member of the original three [[Crinfrid Reavers]], a group of [[Mercenary|mercenaries]] hired by King [[Niedamir]] to take part in the hunt for, [[Myrgtabrakke]] a [[dragon]] outside [[Holopole|Barefield]]. He was known for his expertise with a two-handed sword. He fought alongside [[Gar]] and [[Boholt]], their leader.<ref>{{SoD}}</ref> By the time of 1271, Kennet has seemingly become the leader of the Reavers and addressed Geralt outside [[Camp of Foltest's army|Foltest's army camp]].<ref>{{tw2}}</ref> A few years afterward, the Reavers grew in numbers with Kennet, Boholt, Gar and, to some extent, [[Newboy]] remembered as quite a legends.<ref>{{tw3}} - {{BaW}}</ref>

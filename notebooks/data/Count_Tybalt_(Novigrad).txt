'''Count Tybalt''' was the wealthy host of the high stakes [[Gwent]] tournament at the [[Passiflora]]. He was a calm and balanced nobleman without any indication of racism that was so common in his time.

He uses the [[Monsters Gwent deck|Monsters deck]].

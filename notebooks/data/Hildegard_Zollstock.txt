|Appears_games = {{Tw1}}}}'''Hildegard Zollstock''' was a widow living in the [[Temple Quarter]]. Her husband died roughly a month before {{Year|canon = no|year = 1270}}. She can be found walking on the Graveyard Alley, or standing outside the [[Vizima cemetery|cemetery]]'s gate around noon. It seemed that her deceased counterpart was not happy merely enjoying the great beyond. He was intent on spreading malicious lies about the poor woman.

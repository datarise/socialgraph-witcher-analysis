'''Bea''' was a young woman who worked as a barmaid in [[The Golden Sturgeon]]. She helped [[Ciri]] during her stay in [[Novigrad]] while on the run from the [[Wild Hunt]]. They became friends and when Ciri reappeared in the free city, she visited Bea alongside [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] and gave her a trinket as thanks.

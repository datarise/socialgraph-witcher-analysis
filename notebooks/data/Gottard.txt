'''Gottard''' is peasant living in village [[Yantra]] on the east side of the lake, northeast of [[Novigrad]]. When leader of bandits known as [[Little Red (bandit)|Little Red]] wants to settle score with [[Bertram (peasant)|Bertram]], his neighbor, he is among villagers wanting to somehow protect him. When Geralt appears he is unwelcome at him but then changes his mind and tells everything he know.

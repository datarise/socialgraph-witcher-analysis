'''Dragomir''' was a man that was hanged by the [[Nilfgaardian Empire]] for spying on them. He was accused while he was serving in the [[Nilfgaardian Army]] and was caught eavesdropping on the soldiers and reporting their plans to the [[Redania]]ns. His corpse can be found right next to the location of the quest [[Man's Best Friend]].

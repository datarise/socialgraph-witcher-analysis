'''Genny''' was one of the [[orphans]] that [[Gran]] looked after in [[The Orphans of Crookback Bog|Crookback Bog]] in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}. At one point Genny got a fever but the [[godling]] [[Johnny]] helped cure it.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt freed [[the Ghost in the Tree]]}} Genny, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was saved by the Ghost from the [[Crones]]. The orphans later ended up in [[Novigrad]], and were placed under the care of [[Marabella]] at [[Marabella's School for Tots|her school]].

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt killed the Ghost in the Tree}} Genny, along with all the other orphans from Crookback Bog, was taken to [[Bald Mountain (location)|Aard Cerbin]] by the [[Crones]], where he was killed and consumed by them.

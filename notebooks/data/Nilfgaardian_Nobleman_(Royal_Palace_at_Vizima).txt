This unnamed''' Nilfgaardian nobleman''' is a character in {{Tw3}} temporarily residing in the [[Royal Palace (Vizima)|Royal Palace]] at [[Vizima]], his wealth, power, and social influence put him in an influential position in the court of [[Emhyr var Emreis]].

Players are recommended to play with him as soon as they are able to beat him to obtain his [[gwent]] card as he may not be there later. He plays the [[Nilfgaardian Empire Gwent deck|Nilfgaardian Empire deck]] - a true patriot. If beaten, he awards the player with [[Foltest: The Siegemaster (gwent card)]].

'''Berem''' was a [[lycanthrope]] from around [[Crookback Bog]] in [[Velen]].

==Biography==
===Meeting Geralt===
{{Condition|Text=If [[Ciri]] didn't return to [[Tor Gvalch'ca]]}} in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] returned to the bog to hunt down the last of the three crone sisters, [[Weavess]], and take back Ciri's stolen [[witcher medallion]], which was originally [[Vesemir]]'s. Berem found Geralt in the swamp and introduced himself.

: {{Condition|Text = If Geralt kills Berem}} Geralt decided Berem was too much a threat and killed him to protect the area, and Geralt found Weavess on his own.

: {{Condition|Text = If Geralt allows Berem to live}} Geralt saw that Berem could help him, getting the werewolf to take him through the swamp and lead him to Weavess.

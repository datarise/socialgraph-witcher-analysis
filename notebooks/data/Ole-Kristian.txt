'''Ole-Kristian''' was a [[Skelliger]] man who served at [[Kaer Muire]] with loyalty to [[Clan Drummond]] and Jarl [[Madman Lugos]]. He could have been seen discussing a situation on the isles with another islander by the fire near castle's prison. He feared that, after the death of [[King of the Skellige Isles|King]] [[Bran Tuirseach]], chaos would come and that [[Clan an Craite|an Craites]] would try to attack Kaer Muire.

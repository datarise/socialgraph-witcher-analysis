'''Henry'''<ref>''[[Diplomatic report]]''</ref>''' var Attre''' was a noble who served as a diplomat to [[Novigrad]] for thirteen years, first as consul and later as [[ambassador]]. He lived there in his [[Var Attre Villa]] in the rich [[Gildorf]] district. He informed [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] of the political situation in the [[Northern Kingdoms]] during the [[witcher]]'s visit to [[Royal Palace (Vizima)|Vizima]]. He was the father of both [[Edna var Attre|Edna]] and [[Rosa var Attre]].

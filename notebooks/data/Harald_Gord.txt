'''Harald Gord''' was a skilled [[Dwarf|dwarven]] mercenary at the [[The Witcher 2 Arena|arena]]. [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] could hire him to assist in a fight for 250 {{oren2}}.

At some point during his life he was even hired by [[Carlo Varese]], the leader of a [[Novigrad]]ian gang known as the [[Crownsplitters]].

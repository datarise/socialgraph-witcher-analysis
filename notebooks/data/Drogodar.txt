'''Drogodar''' was a grey-haired [[bard]] at the royal court of [[Cintra]] in service of [[Queen]] [[Calanthe]] where he was popular among nobility. His instrument of choice was the lute.

== {{Hexer}} ==
In first movie and TV series, Drogodar was played by Polish actor [https://pl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dariusz_Siastacz Dariusz Siastacz] and sang ballad about Calanthe's deed during [[Battle of Hochebuz]].

=== Episodes ===
* 6: "[[Calanthe (episode)|Calanthe]]"

== {{Tw3}} ==
He appeared only briefly in third game where he performed a ballad about [[Cerys an Craite]]'s encounter with the [[hym]]. Later on, he was among the victims of the [[Berserker (creature)|Berserker]] attack.

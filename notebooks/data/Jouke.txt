'''Jouke''' was a [[Redania]]n archer serving on [[HMS Oxenfurt-Tretogor]] to protect King [[Radovid V]] during {{Tw3}}.

Throughout the [[Reason of State]] side quest, he and his comrades, [[Caspar]], [[Damiaan]], [[Freek]], and the witch hunter [[Egbert]] are commanded by King Radovid to stay near the gate to [[St. Gregory's Bridge]] and secure the area. He died from a shot by a [[Temeria]]n partisan.

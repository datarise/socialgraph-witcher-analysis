The '''Quartermaster''' lives in the upper part of [[Crow's Perch]] next to the armorers [[Fergus Graem]] and [[Yoana]]. [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] can play [[Gwent]] with him and buy some cards. He also sells the [[Nilfgaardian Armor set]] and the [[Nilfgaardian crossbow]] once the free DLC has been installed.

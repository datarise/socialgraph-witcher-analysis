'''Sofus the Bull''' is a fistfighter from [[Metinna]] that [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] met in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, when he was looking for ushers in the [[Novigrad Docks]]. Geralt can either pay 70{{Crown Item}}or fight the two brothers and they will agree to usher for the play. If Geralt loses the fight they will still usher, but he will need to pay 140{{Crown Item}}.

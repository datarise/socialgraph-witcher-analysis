'''Fenn''' was a member of the [[Blue Stripes]], an elite [[Temeria]]n Special Forces Unit in {{Year|canon = no|year = 1271}}.

He enjoyed a good drink, [[arm wrestling]] and [[The Witcher 2 fistfighting|fistfighting]].

Fenn shared the fate of the Blue Stripes and died by the order of [[Detmold|Dethmold]].

== Trivia ==
* Fenn's body is missing from the tent where the Blue Stripes were hanged. Same goes for [[Thirteen]] and [[Silas]]. It's unknown whether this was intended or an oversight.

'''Sambor''' was a [[The Witcher 2 merchants|merchant]], [[The Witcher 2 dice poker|dice poker player]], and (with patch 1.2 installed) [[Barbers and Coiffeuses|barber]] who can be found in the [[camp followers' encampment]] during chapter II.

== [[The Witcher 2 journal|Journal]] Entry ==
:''The saying "No sweets without sweat" was certainly true in Sambor's case. This trader with a proverb for every occasion ran a thriving pawn shop, selling interesting scrolls, books and manuscripts among other items.''
:''[[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] was surprised to learn that Sambor was also a skillful barber. For a fistful or [[oren]]s, the trader could work wonders with the [[witcher]]'s mane.''

'''Black Bogdan''' was a [[horse]] racer outside [[Crow's Perch]].

No matter what order he's raced against, he'll always give [[horse blinders]] and 40 {{Crown}} when beaten. He may also have either obstacle appear (loose barrels or log) when racing him.

'''Sigurd''' was a [[Blacksmiths|blacksmith]] from [[Arinbjorn]]. When he has a free time he is either playing [[gwent]] or writing poems. He sells [[The Witcher 3 crafting components|crafting components]], [[Runestone|runestones]], and a number of [[:Category:The Witcher 3 crafting diagrams|crafting diagrams]].

==Trivia==
* The name could be a reference to the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sigurd legendary hero] in Norse mythology.

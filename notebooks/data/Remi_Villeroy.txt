'''Remi Villeroy''' was a [[mage]] from the [[Northern Kingdoms]], who went into hiding in the [[Farcorners]] district of [[Novigrad]] with his wife, Louisa, and his adoptive son.

== Biography ==
In {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, after the [[witch hunts]] broke out, he went on the run from the [[witch hunter]]s. Officially, they wanted him for contacting the [[Lodge of Sorceresses|Lodge of Sorceresses]] via a [[megascope]], and poisoning the minds of [[Redania]]n soldiers.

If [[Geralt of Rivia|Geralt]] intervened and helped Remi and his family, the mage thanked him and expressed a desire to give up magic before rewarding Geralt with [[perun runestone|Perun]], [[Veles runestone|Veles]], and [[Chernobog runestone|Chernobog]] [[runestone]]s.

'''Mael''' is a secondary character in {{Tw2}}. He can be found in [[Vergen]] during chapter II.

This quiet elf is a [[The Witcher 2 merchants|merchant]] and a [[Craftsman]]. His shop is situated on the left side of [[Rhundurin Square]]. Mael is also a [[The Witcher 2 dice poker|dice player]] but he is not involved in any quests. You can ask him if you need to craft any kind of [[The Witcher 2 traps|traps]], [[The Witcher 2 weapons|swords]] or [[The Witcher 2 armor|armor]].

The '''merchant''' from [[Claywich]] does not appear there until he is freed from the [[Bandits' Camp]] located on a large island to the east of [[Oreton]]. Initially, he is marked on the map as a [[Person(s) in distress|person in distress]]. Once freed, the merchant can be found in Claywich, where he plays [[gwent]], using the [[Northern Realms Gwent deck|Northern Realms deck]], and sells some gwent cards.

'''Yorg''' was a man that, in {{Year|canon=no|year=1272}}, was chained up to a rock in [[Skellige]], having been accused of killing his uncle, [[Gretter]], after [[Yorg's sister|his sister]] told him Gretter had molested her. However, this was actually a lie and his sister simply didn't like the uncle and thought he stank, not realizing Yorg would kill him.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt frees him}} he will thank the [[witcher]], promising to repay him should they ever cross paths again.

{{Condition|Text = If Geralt leaves him to his fate}} Yorg soon died from the punishment he was sentenced to.

'''La Valette Guard Captain''' was an old and experienced soldier who served [[Baroness]] [[Maria Louisa La Valette]] as commander of her guard around her [[Baroness la Valette's Villa|villa]] in [[Novigrad]].

==Biography==
In {{Year|year=1272|canon=no}}, he met [[Geralt of Rivia]] when the [[witcher]] came looking for Maria Louisa. While he originally objected to their meet, Maria and [[Morvran Voorhis]] came out of their own accord to meet Geralt.

== Associated Quest ==
* [[Broken Flowers]]

== Notes ==
* Unlike the other guards in the game, you are unable to attack the captain or members of the La Valette guard.

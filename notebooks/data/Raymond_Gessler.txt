'''Raymond Gessler''' is a secondary character in {{Tw2}}. He is a [[The Witcher 2 merchants|merchant]] and [[The Witcher 2 dice poker|dice poker player]] who can be found in the [[camp followers' encampment]] during chapter II. With patch 1.3 installed, he also provides [[The Witcher 2 storage|storage]] services.

== Journal Entry ==
:''The prophet [[Lebioda]]'s wisdom, as recorded in the Good Book, was Raymond Gessler's recipe for life. This food peddler looked to the prophet's teachings at every step, following them even in pursuing his enterprise. This worked surprisingly well.''

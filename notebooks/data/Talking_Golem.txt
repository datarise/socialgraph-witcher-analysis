'''Talking Golem''' is a [[golem]] created by [[Dearhenna]] to Guard his laboratory and encountered in [[The Secrets of Loc Muinne]].

It was created with a greater degree of intelligence, autonomy and judgement than normal golems.

The golem's role was to greet "guests" and eliminate "intruders" to Dearhenna's laboratory.

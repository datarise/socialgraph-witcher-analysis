The '''Grossbart Brothers''' were a trio of [[Skellige]]r brothers who murdered, raped, and burned down homes so severely a bounty was put out on them in {{Year|canon = no|year = 1272}}, drawing the attention of [[Djenge Frett]], a [[bounty hunter]] in the area. At the time, the three were camping out in the [[Grotto (Ard Skellig)|Grotto]] on [[Ard Skellig]].
